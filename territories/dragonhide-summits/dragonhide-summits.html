Dragonhide Summits

The Dragonhide Summits are to the South of the mainland o Miravalis, covered in an open landscape of arid savannah littered with incredibly tall and jagged spires of rock and ash. This is the only region on the planet Miravalis to comfortably home dragons.

In the planet of Miravalis, dragons are powerful and respected, but are not as powerful or mythical as in other universes, taking one small step towards becoming something like dinosaurs or immensely large reptiles rather than world altering entities.

Nevertheless, these dragons still pose an incredible threat to humanoid lives, however, people still live in the Dragonhide Summits unharmed. After creating small towns, inhabitants of these lands would have their livelihoods swiftly demolished by a nearby dragon or wyvern, but the inhabitants themselves were never the target.

It was learned by the people of the Dragonhide Summits that the dragons do not mind the intrusion of pesky humanoids, but instead were disgusted by the desecration of their savannah through manmade buildings. Since then, the people of the summits have adapted, primarily living in moving caravans and closely knit enclaves of family and friends. Very few enclaves have even formed agreements with dragons, living in their dens that are beneath the spiraling craggy peaks of the summits. Some criticize this life, but the people of the summits are the freest of anyone on the planet.
