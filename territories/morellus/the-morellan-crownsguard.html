The Morellan Crownsguard

Five incredibly strong, yet balanced and wise members of Morellan Society. Crownsguard are often tasked with law enforcement tasks, but are also responsible for taking on a pillar for Morellan Society. Past projects of Crownsguard include Amplicite and Detricite Engineering, City Reconstruction post Unbounding, Monitoring of Gnoxrus, Foreign Diplomacy, etc.

The Crownsguard are a group of legendary individuals, talented in skillful in the art of combat, decision making, and development in Morellus. The Crownsguard were initially assembled by King Ganon Luminara in the year 1739, marking a large transition in Morellus, and allowed for mages to be apart of Morellus's mightiest defenders. The founding of the Crownsguard replaced the former elite group, theThe Formidable Five (former), signifying a draw towards progression, rather than violent and intimidating oppression.

The current Crownsguard, and the faces of the Morellan government are listed below.

Wolfe Trigon - Harengon - Horizon Walker Ranger

  • Wolfe is an excellent asset to the Crownsguard, and is primarily responsible for any missions requiring espionage or infiltrating. Maneuvering through the planes is no easy task, but Wolfe does it with ease. He is equipped with a long ranged, high-caliber Amplicite rifle, and is extremely skilled with long range takedowns.

Azehra Olm - Satyr - College of Glamour Bard

  • Azehra is the face of diplomacy for Morellus, as well as the main public representative for the Crownsguard. She is incredibly charming, but do not let that fool you. Her silver tongue and cunning intellect makes her quite the inspirer, and is often tasked with inspiring large squadrons of Morellan troops in wartime, along with her fellow Crownsguard.

Wayara Quinn - Human - Bladesinger Wizard

  • Wayara is the perfect balance of defense and offense in the Crownsguard, but is mainly tasked with crowd control. She utilizes her speed to act as a search and rescue in any mass casualty events. Using her incredible knowledge over the arcane, she is often seen at the Grand Morellan University, helping further the subjects taught and paying homage to where she herself learned her wizardry.

Veroah Brao - Amethyst Dragonborn - Battle Master Fighter

  • Veroah is of the most versatile in the group, and comes from a long line of valiant fighters in Morellus. Their parents before them, and the grandparents before them all have a lineage in serving in the Crownsguard, and even the Formidable Five during its existence. They are incredibly strong and fierce on the field of combat, but they also train a large portion of the Ivory Warden, teaching them values of when and why to fight, instead of simply how to.

Bjork Heyes - Bugbear - Totem Warrior Barbarian

  • Bowne, despite his name, is not your stereotypical barbarian. Incredibly well-versed in battle strategy, Bowne serves as the primary tactician of the Crownsguard in wartime, as well as an immoveable frontline beast. As for his contribution to Morellan society, he mainly spends his off time working alongside Morellan engineers in the Amplicite Canyons, helping move and excavate large regions of stone.
