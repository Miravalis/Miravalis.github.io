Old Stones Geography

The geography of the Old Stones is some of the most diverse in the planet, both naturally and magically. From thick and humid rainforests, to open plains and woodland forests, and incredibly tall mountain ranges, the Old Stones offers a varying terrain.

One odd geographic phenomena to the Old Stones - almost the entirety of the region is placed under a Wild Magic Zone, with the heaviest of effects coming from the center, Lake Arbor, said to be the origin point of all magic in the material plane. Many weird, inexplicable things happen at this lake, and some say if you glance long enough at your reflection in its water, you won't ever look away.

Furthermore, some of The High Scholars of the Old Stones reckon the immense magical power of this region comes from tears in planar reality - with large dumps of fey magic pouring in from the feywilds from multiple rifts. These rifts were not always here, but were observed to open in the mid 400s. These are monitored by the The Grovewatchers, but can serve as a real threat to the people of the stones.
