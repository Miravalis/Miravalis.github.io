Rexian Culture

Strength means everything to Rexia and its people, and so, its culture is built entirely around the abstract concept. To the Rexian people, strength is your ability to protect yourself and your companions. But do not get this confused with a brutish society of axe slinging meatheads. Rexian's value strength in all forms, whether it be harnessed from blade, spellbook, or innate intellect.

The biggest staple of Rexian culture is the concept of trial by combat, which has been integrated into both Rexian culture and Rexian government. It is customary for every town to have an arena within it to hold gladiator combat, whether it be for fun, or for citizenship purposes, as where you live is determined primarily on strength.

All is fair in Rexia, and the only thing that quantifies you is your strength, which makes Rexia a sort of dystopia, if you will. There is no prejudice based off your weapon of choice, race, or family name in Rexia, but only your strength.

The one thing that is protected and sacred in Rexia, though, is the young and elderly. Children and the elderly have the decision to live in the Forgiving Lands, a peninsula to the South of Rexia with the most tolerable climate conditions, and strict rules on raids. Anyone who disrespects this tradition does not live to desecrate it again.
